#fashionForward

Forecasting Fashion in the next decade

2025-2035

Fashion trends mirror the world we live in.

They reflect cultural shifts, political movements, and technological progress through clothing.

Cultural & Social

Fashion, once dictated by royalty, is now driven by social media, film, music, and television. In today’s fast-paced cycle of microtrends, some have crossed the line into cultural appropriation.

Political & Economic

Politics and economics shape fashion by affecting affordability, material access, and cultural expression. Economic shifts lead to practical styles, while political movements inspire statement pieces.

Technology & Innovation

Technology drives fashion through smart textiles, new materials, and digital tools, enabling greater creativity, customization, and function.
